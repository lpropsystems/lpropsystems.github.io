About Us

We're a small group of people building small scale rockets. We started as Langley Company in late 2018, building model rockets and developing avionics systems. Since then, we've changed our name and our goal. We are now LPS, or Langley Propulsion Systems (LPROP for short). Join the convo on Discord at lprop.tk.
